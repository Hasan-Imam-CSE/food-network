Moong Dal

Moong dal beans are dried, mature mung beans that have been skinned. Naturally low in fat and high in fibre, a 1-cup serving of cooked moong dal has less than 1 gram of total fat, over 14 grams of protein and 15.4 grams of dietary fibre. A 1-cup serving contains 212 calories.
